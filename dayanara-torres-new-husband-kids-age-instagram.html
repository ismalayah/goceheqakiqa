Dayanara Torres New Husband, Kids, Age, Instagram

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  170 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Puerto Rican actress and former Miss Universe, Dayanara Torres, is currently unmarried and now lives in Florida.

There are no records of any new relationship in her life.

In December 2018, Dayanara got engaged to Hollywood movie producer, Louis D’Esposito. He eventually broke up with her after she was diagnosed of skin cancer.

Torres was previously married to singer Marc Anthony and they had two sons together, Cristian Anthony Muñiz, and Ryan Anthony Muñiz.

The couple divorced in the summer of 2002 after a stormy relationship, later reconciled and renewed their vows in a ceremony held in Puerto Rico in December 2002.

In January 2004 the former Miss Universe filed for another divorce from Marc, which was final.

Dayanara Torres Kids

Torres has two kids – Cristian Anthony Muñiz, born on February 5, 2001; and Ryan Anthony Muñiz, born on August 16, 2003.

Dayanara Torres Age

Dayanara Torres was born on October 28, 1974. She is 47 years old.

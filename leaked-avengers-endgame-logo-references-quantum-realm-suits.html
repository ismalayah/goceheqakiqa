New Avengers: Endgame Logo Teases The Quantum Realm Suits

 Posted on September 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  284 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In the wake of the first Avengers: Endgame trailer dropping last month, Marvel Studios unveiled the film’s first official logo and poster, both of which were purple-tinted in a clear nod to Thanos. This recently leaked logo, however, has a lot more to do with the heroes of the saga than the villain who so brutally defeated them last year.

The big red, black, and silver ‘A’ artwork reportedly emerged along with the set of leaked action figure photos that were shared online this weekend, and its design seems to be modeled on the shiny new ‘Quantum Realm suits’ that several characters are seen wearing in the pics.

As it stands, we’ve yet to get any confirmation that the spacey new outfits worn by the likes of Thor, Black Widow, Rocket and Hulk are actually designed for trips into the Quantum Realm. Still, much of the internet is already assuming as such – hence the armor’s unofficial name – and at the very least, it’s clear that this mysterious place will be of great significance in the Avengers: Infinity War sequel.

Regardless, these suits are just one of several revelations that the recent stream of leaks has brought us. In the various unofficial material that dropped over the weekend, we can also find early glimpses at the costumes worn by Pepper Potts and Clint Barton in their respective new personas of Rescue and Ronin. Better yet, one action figure leak has seemingly confirmed the comeback of an old fan favorite from one of Thor’s previous adventures. We’ll see who else is returning to the fray, and what they’ll be wearing along the way, when Avengers: Endgame hits theaters on April 26th, 2019.
